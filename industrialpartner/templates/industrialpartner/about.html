Founded in November 1997, Industrial Partner, a Relevant Technologies Corporation , has grown into a leading provider of industrial computing, automation, and networking solutions. Industrial Partner designs and delivers components and systems used in the most ruggedized environments found in commercial, manufacturing, industrial, military and aerospace applications. Industrial Partner serves a diverse market that extends beyond the Americas to Asia, Europe and Africa.

Industrial Partner recognizes the importance of time to market and is focused on delivering cost effective and timely solutions. Building strategic relationships with manufacturing partners is one of its core strategies. Through its resourceful engineering, research and development efforts, Industrial Partner continues to develop innovative solutions that address the ever-changing and challenging problems facing its partners and clients.
